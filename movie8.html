This page is about The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift, the third film in the Fast & Furious series.

Title The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift
Director Justin Lin
Producer Neal H. Moritz
Leading Actor Lucas Black (Sean Boswell)
Leading Actress Nathalie Kelley (Neela)
Rating PG-13
Description High school troublemaker Sean Boswell is sent to Tokyo to live with his father. There he discovers Japan's underground drift-racing scene and challenges the local champion, Takashi (DK). Directed by Justin Lin and produced by Neal H. Moritz, the film is known for its mountain and city drift sequences and for expanding the franchise with a new setting and style.
